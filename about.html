ORGANISATION OVERVIEW

Guiding stars mentorship Academy is a rising startup, established in 2024 with a mission to bridge the gap between marketing academia and practice. We create an inclusive and diverse community where aspiring marketing students, graduates, and professionals can build knowledge, gain confidence and develop the skills necessary to lead in the global marketing industry. Our focus on continous learning and talent development forms the core of our comprehensive mentorship program

As we grow, we remain commited to delivering impactifull marketing solutions and nurturing lasting relationships with our clients and partners. Our goal is to empower our team to reach their full potential and contribute to a brighter, more sustainable future in the marketing world, embodying the spirit of starlight guidance.

Our Mission

To Foster a Dynamic Learning Environment Where Aspiring Marketing Students and Graduates will Build Knowledge to Bridge the Gap Between Academia and Practice, Cultivate Confidence, Character and Skilss to Lead in the Marketing Space Globally.

Our Vision

To Elevate, Educate, Empower and Transform Marketers Futures Together.

DIVERSITY AND Inclusion Statement

At Guiding Stars, we believe that a diverse and inclusive environment is not just a goal, but a journey of continual improvement and learning. Our commitment to diversity and inclusion is embedded in our core values and is a critical component of our mentorship program.

Our mentorship program is designed to bridge gaps,promotes understanding, and leverage diverse viewpoints as a source of strength and innovation. We are committed to providing equal opportunities for all, ensuring that our mentors and mentees represent the varied fabric of our workforce.

Inclusion for us means proactive engagement, listening to diverse perspectives and integrating this richness of experience into the fabric of our organization. We strive to ensure that all members of our community, regardless of their background, feel supported, valued, and connected.

At Guiding Stars, diversity and inclusion are not just words but actions we live by every day. We are on a journey, continuously learning, evolving, and striving to be better. We invite you to join us in this endeavour as we grow together.
